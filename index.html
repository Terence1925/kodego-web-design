Remember to warm up before each workout by performing some dynamic stretches or light cardio exercises. Additionally, adjust the weights and repetitions based on your current strength and fitness level. Focus on maintaining good form and gradually increase the intensity as you progress.

Rest days are crucial for muscle recovery, so make sure to include them in your routine. Proper nutrition and hydration are also essential for achieving your goals, so be sure to fuel your body with a balanced diet and stay hydrated throughout the day.

Lastly, listen to your body and modify the exercises or take additional rest days if needed. It's important to prioritize safety and avoid injury.
